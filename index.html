about us

Saipem is a global energy powerhouse that engineers and builds the world's most complex offshore and onshore infrastructure,

bridging the gap between traditional oil and gas excellence and a high-tech, sustainable future.

SAIPEM'S SERVICES

Offshore Engineering & Construction

Provides end-to-end EPCI (Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Installation) for subsea pipelines (SURF), fixed platforms, and floating facilities. It utilizes a world-class vessel fleet to manage complex projects in deepwater and harsh environments.

Onshore Engineering & Construction

Specializes in the design and building of massive land-based energy plants, including LNG liquefaction, refineries, and chemical facilities. It focuses on modular construction and is increasingly pivoting toward hydrogen and carbon capture plants.

Offshore Drilling Services

Operates a high-tech fleet of drillships, jack-ups, and semi-submersibles. This division performs exploration and production drilling for major oil companies in environments ranging from shallow shelf waters to ultra-deepwater.

Onshore Drilling Services

Manages a large fleet of land rigs (ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 HP) that operate in diverse terrains like deserts, jungles, and mountains. They provide specialized services such as geothermal drilling and unconventional gas extraction.

OUR MISSION

SAIPEM'S COMMITMENTS

decarbonization of traditional energy sectors while expanding its leadership in renewable sectors like offshore

wind, hydrogen, and carbon capture.This commitment is underpinned by a "zero-tolerance" approach to safety and corruption,
